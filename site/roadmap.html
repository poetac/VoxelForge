SCOPE

Scope expansion (~9–12 months out)

Long-term

Three-step staircase from rocket-only: propulsion (air-breathing entry: ramjet → turbojet → turbofan → scramjet → RBCC) → power generation (gas turbine → steam → ICE → fuel cell) → general computational engineering. Architectural finding: ~40 % of voxelforge reuses as-is for any new engine family; ~60 % net-new per family. Recommended shape: parallel pillar (no upfront platform refactor). Trigger: S 37 ships AND ≥3 hot-fire readiness items done.

AirBreathingPowerGenGeneralCE

Evaluated and declined

Items consciously not on the roadmap.

Considered during the 2026-04-22 pre-production audit and 2026-04-23 audits, declined with rationale. Do not re-evaluate without new evidence.

  • Unified IEngineResult supertype — dispatch fits cleanly in a ~40-LOC branch inside Evaluate. Revisit only if a third engine topology appears.
  • SIMD vectorisation and double → float — deferred per the performance audit; no cost justification today.
  • GPU voxelisation (CUDA / HLSL compute shader for PicoGK SDF) — determinism risk: shader FP rounding ≠ CPU, breaking byte-identical round-trip.
  • Differentiable physics / full autodiff — physics-fidelity rewrite cost vastly exceeds value over CMA-ES + multi-chain SA. Bartz + Dittus-Boelter empirical polytropes don't port cleanly to dual numbers.
  • Thermoacoustic instability solver — empirical, nonlinear, data-starved. One false-negative is catastrophic false confidence. Existing StabilityScreening covers chugging + JANNAF screen.
  • LPBF slicer integration (vendor API) — vendor-locked, license-blocked. Better as a partnership than an in-house feature. S 27 printability gates cover the DfM screen.
