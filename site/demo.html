Multi-chain SA · default-on since 2026-04-27

The trace below is a single-chain replay for clarity. Production SA now runs N independent chains in lockstep with a Barrier-enforced elite migration, plus per-chain Sobol slices for low-discrepancy initial coverage. Auto-scales to ProcessorCount − 2 clamped 1–16. Strict determinism: same seed + chain count → identical result. Promoted to default 2026-04-27 after the cascade benchmarks: RL-10 EXPANDER 89.8 % → 31 %, merlin INJ_FACE 100 % → 32 %, pintle PINTLE_BLOCKAGE 99.9 % → 69 % @ 500-iter. T1.5 progressive-fidelity pre-screen now layered on top, rejecting ~60–80 % of candidates at ~10 µs each before paying the thermal solve.

Canonical-preset feasibility · post-A-2 (2026-04-30)

From zero feasible to four.

Pre-cascade, every canonical preset returned 0 / N feasible candidates. After 16 PRs of cascade fixes plus AutoSeeder hardening (Sprint A-2, 2026-04-30), four of five presets reach feasibility. pressure-fed-small remains intentionally infeasible (1 kN regen regime out of scope).

What you're looking at (left)

A schematic, not the real STL.

The cross-section is an SVG approximation of examples/small-pf-5kn/: convergent-divergent contour, helical regen channels (96 of them), coaxial swirl injector face, mounting flange. The real STL has 0.15 mm voxel resolution and an order of magnitude more geometric detail. This demo is for intuition; the file you'd actually print lives in the repo.

What you're looking at (right)

A real SA replay.

Every dot is a candidate evaluated by the optimiser. Orange dots are gate-rejected — feasibility violations the cascade caught before they became expensive voxel work. Green dots are admissible candidates the SA accepted or rejected on objective alone. The descending line traces best-so-far chamber mass.
