What it does

Mesh-based CAD struggles with what additive manufacturing actually unlocks.

Conformal TPMS coolant channels. Monolithic fusion of chamber, turbopump, and feed manifold into a single part. Aerospike plug cooling with internally-routed regen passages. voxelforge runs on a voxel-implicit kernel (PicoGK 2.2.0) where those operations are boolean-trivial. A coolant channel isn't a swept surface — it's the negative of a Schwarz P field clipped to a chamber shell.

Combustion & thermo

Equilibrium-corrected propellant tables, Bartz hot-gas heat flux, atomisation SMD, Crocco N-τ stability screen, start-transient simulation.

  • LOX/CH4 · LOX/H2 · LOX/RP-1
  • Chug · buzz · screech screening
  • Film cooling + ablative fallback

Coolant & heat transfer

Dittus-Boelter, Gnielinski, Sieder-Tate plus TPMS-channel variants. Full regen solver with chilldown transient. Dean-number Nu for helical channels.

  • H2 · CH4 · RP-1 coolant fluids
  • Haaland LPBF roughness friction
  • Purge-flow modelling

Geometry & manufacturing

Implicit chamber contours, aerospike plugs, preburners, turbine impellers. TPMS channels generated directly from implicits. Build-orientation advisor.

  • Schwarz P · Gyroid TPMS
  • Coax · pintle · impinging · swirl
  • STL · 3MF · CFD-field export

Feed system

Full propellant-line pressure stackup. Line-loss, valve Cv, filter catalogues. Turbopump and turbine sizing with shaft critical-speed check.

  • Pressure-fed · electric pump
  • Gas-gen · staged · expander · ORSC · tap-off
  • Auto-routed feed manifolds

Optimisation

Simulated annealing over a unified design record. Pareto-front tracking, run provenance, auto-seeder, solver-warning collation.

  • 34-D continuous + categorical
  • 196 feasibility gates as admission
  • Deterministic — same seed, same STL

Verification

Every shipped sprint has a frozen JSONL fingerprint baseline. The post-cascade diff is the cascade's measured impact — physics changes are tracked, not assumed.

  • 5 canonical-design SA fingerprints
  • JSONL schema v1 (ADR-013)
  • Bench-regression CI on every PR

Pipeline

From parameters to printable STL.

01 / INPUT
Operating conditions

Thrust, chamber pressure, mixture ratio, propellant pair, ambient.

02 / SEED
Auto-seeder

Bell geometry, channel count, L*, MR — physics-grounded starting point.

03 / SEARCH
SA + 196 gates

34-D simulated annealing. Every candidate clears 196 admission criteria before scoring.

04 / VOXELISE
PicoGK kernel

Implicit SDF for shell, channels, flanges, bosses. Boolean-trivial composition.

05 / EXPORT
STL · 3MF · CFD

Single STL ready for laser powder-bed fusion. Plus build sheet, safety report, CFD field.

Design philosophy

Most preliminary-design tools optimise first and validate second. voxelforge inverts that: physics reality is an admission criterion, not a penalty term. The scoring function only ever sees designs that are already buildable, cool-able, startable, and structurally sound.
